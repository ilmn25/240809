StoneHatchet = Cutting 1 (minimum tree-harvest cutting tool)
StonePickaxe = Mining 1 (minimum stone/ore mining tool)

Hammer = Building 3
DiamondAxe = Cutting 4

## Exact Step-by-Step Station Progression

> Logs are not available until you have a chopping tool. Unless logs are explicitly dropped on the floor, you must craft a hatchet first.

1. Gather 5 Flint and 4 Sticks from floor loot.
2. Use any floor-spawned Log pieces if available; otherwise, you cannot progress to Station/Workbench yet because logs require a cutting tool.
3. Craft a Station using 15 Log + 5 Flint once you have 15 Log.
4. At the Station, craft a Workbench using 15 Log.
5. At the Workbench, craft a StoneHatchet using 2 Sticks + 3 Flint.
6. Use the StoneHatchet to chop trees and gather the Logs needed for all later builds. This is the minimum cutting tool for tree harvest.
7. At the Workbench, craft a StonePickaxe using 2 Sticks + 4 Flint.
8. At the Workbench, craft a Hammer using 2 Sticks + 2 Flint.
9. Use the StonePickaxe to mine 15 Gravel and 9 MetalChunks. This is the minimum mining tool for stone/ore mining.
10. Gather 15 Log with the StoneHatchet.
11. Craft a Campfire using 4 Gravel + 1 Flint + 15 Log.
12. Use the Campfire to craft 18 Charcoal from 36 Log (2 Log each).
13. Use the StonePickaxe to mine 36 more Gravel.
14. At the Station, craft a Furnace using 5 Log + 15 Gravel.
15. At the Furnace, craft 6 Slag using 18 Gravel + 12 Charcoal.
16. At the Furnace, craft 1 Steel using 3 MetalChunks + 2 Charcoal.
17. Gather 5 Log with the StoneHatchet.
18. At the Station, craft a Sawmill using 1 Steel + 6 Slag + 5 Log.
19. Gather 15 Log with the StoneHatchet.
20. At the Sawmill, craft 5 Plank using 15 Log.
21. Use the StonePickaxe to mine 9 more MetalChunks.
22. Use the Campfire to craft 6 more Charcoal from 12 Log.
23. At the Furnace, craft 6 more Slag using 18 Gravel + 12 Charcoal.
24. At the Furnace, craft 2 more Steel using 6 MetalChunks + 4 Charcoal.
25. At the Station, craft a Stonecutter using 2 Steel + 6 Slag + 5 Plank.

### Why tools are required first
- Gravel and MetalChunks cannot be mined reliably without the StonePickaxe.
- The Hammer is needed for block breaking and resource gathering in many early-game areas.
- The Workbench is required before you can craft the StonePickaxe, Hammer, Furnace, Sawmill, and Stonecutter.

### What this sequence unlocks
- After step 3: Station is craftable.
- After step 4: Workbench is craftable at the Station.
- After step 8: Campfire is craftable.
- After step 11: Furnace is craftable at the Station.
- After step 15: Sawmill is craftable at the Station.
- After step 22: Stonecutter is craftable at the Station.
